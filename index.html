Introduction

The COVID- 19 pandemic across the globe is atypical. The Corona virus has brought the unpredicted fate in the 21st century. Something that has never happened in the 75-year history of the United Nations. As we all know, we were unprepared for this sort of catastrophic phase. The virus made a serious impact on the human race. People lost their belove ones, struggled for their lives and to sustain. However, most of the countries managed to survive the pandemic with the experiences they had with similar or minor outbreaks and also by following certain standards and protocols suggested by the health professionals and WHO.

Project Constraints

Scope, Cost and Time

Scope

The scope of this project spreads from researching and understanding the pandemic to construct a functional plan and deliverables which will make the Fraser Valley to defeat another outbreak. A dedicated team will be hired to study and research the whole situation and also to learn how different countries handled and overcome this pandemic globally. Furthermore, surveys will be conducted to know what people in the Fraser Valley thinks about what went wrong, and how this pandemic has affected their lives. Concurrently, based on our experiences we gained through surviving the situation from 2019 will be considered and build a system where we will be ready with enough medical facilities and medical professionals, strategies which will make sure our daily livelihoods are unaffected.

Time

The project has a completion date on Dec 31,2023. The whole project will be divided into smaller components with an individual deadline which will be delivered as per schedule.

Cost

The main investor to this project will be our Canadian Government. The government has agreed to invest 100 million dollars to the project which will be utilizing for the successful running of the project. More investors are expected to join this huge project as it benefits and can make a great change during this pandemic.
